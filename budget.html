// Import

Import Bank CSV

Export a transaction CSV from your bank/card and upload it here. Nothing is sent anywhere except straight into your own Firestore — no third-party bank connection involved.

Importing from all 4 accounts? A payment from Navy Federal to a card shows up on both statements — once as an outgoing transfer, once as an incoming payment. Counting both sides doubles it. Pick one side to record as the real "SavorOne payment" / "OneKey payment" (Navy Federal's outgoing side is usually easiest), and set that same transaction's category to — Skip (do not import) — on the card statement's side so it isn't counted twice.

Valid categories: income.paycheck, income.rental1, income.rental2, income.vaDisability, income.other, purdue.groceries, purdue.gas, purdue.dining, purdue.prescription, purdue.clothing, purdue.pet, purdue.entertainment, purdue.amazon, purdue.travel, purdue.misc, fixed.utilities, fixed.phone, fixed.insurance, fixed.pet, fixed.subscriptions, fixed.rent, fixed.mortgage, fixed.truck, fixed.otherFixed, debt.savorOne, debt.oneKey, debt.interest. These rules just pre-fill a best guess — you'll review and can change every transaction's category individually in the next step.
